Language Proficiency Courses

We offer comprehensive preparation for these essential language tests to help you achieve your immigration goals.

PTE (Pearson Test of English)

The Pearson Test of English (PTE) is a computer-based academic English language test aimed at non-native English speakers wanting to study abroad. It tests Reading, Writing, Listening and Speaking.

Our PTE preparation program includes:

  • Full-length practice tests with detailed score reports
  • Personalized feedback on speaking and writing tasks
  • Strategies for each question type in all test sections
  • Vocabulary building and grammar refinement sessions
  • Mock interviews and speaking practice with native speakers

IELTS (International English Language Testing System)

IELTS is the world's most popular English language proficiency test for higher education and global migration, with over 3 million tests taken in the past year.

Our IELTS program features:

  • Comprehensive coverage of Academic and General Training modules
  • Expert-led workshops for each test component
  • Weekly practice tests under exam conditions
  • One-on-one
  • One-on-one speaking practice with certified examiners
  • Writing task evaluation with band score prediction

TOEFL (Test of English as a Foreign Language)

The TOEFL test measures your ability to use and understand English at the university level and evaluates how well you combine your reading, listening, speaking and writing skills.

Our TOEFL preparation includes:

  • Full diagnostic test to identify strengths and weaknesses
  • Integrated skills practice for the iBT format
  • Time management strategies for each section
  • Vocabulary enhancement focused on academic English
  • Simulated test environment with performance analysis
